Urology

Urology focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of urinary tract diseases in both males and females, addressing conditions affecting the kidneys, bladder, ureters, urethra, and prostate.

About:

Dr. Ankit Kayal is a Consultant Urologist & Andrologist, Advanced Laparoscopic & Kidney Transplant Surgeon at Bombay Hospital, Jaipur. He holds an MBBS, MS, and MCh in Urology and has received prestigious fellowships, including the Advanced Endourology Fellowship from Shanghai University. Actively involved in research, he has numerous publications in reputed medical journals covering urological diseases and surgical advancements. His contributions to urology have been recognized with multiple awards, including the World Endourology Society Summer Student Scholarship and robotic surgery training. Dr. Kayal is also a member of esteemed urological societies, reflecting his dedication to excellence in patient care and medical research.
